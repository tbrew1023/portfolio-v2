Sonic GX

Sonic the Hedgehog fan game

Project Summary

Run, jump, and spin your way to the chaos emeralds before Dr. Robotnik takes over the island!

Creative Process

I began my software development journey building hundreds of games with game maker as a kid, which allowed me to grasp the fundamentals of development and design through conditionals, event driven interactions, objects, and graphics rendering.

Results

Sonic GX was the first complete game I'd ever developed, and was released around 2011. The game got over 1,400 plays on yoyogames.com before the site was shut down, but I had so much fun making this game and I can't wait to jump back into designing a new platformer.
