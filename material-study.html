Material design study

Research study on Google's Material Design

Project Summary

The purpose of this project was to identify the origins of Google's Material Design philosphy, from the history of skeuomorphic design, to the universal adoption of flat design and how Material Design attempts to combine both of these concepts. This presentation aims to deliniate why Material Design works so well, how to use Material Design, and why designers and developers should be using it.

Creative Process

This presentation was made with Google Slides, but I made sure to utilize meaningful motion in the form of transition animations and gifs to not only maintain a sense of spacial visualization, but also to exemplify the philosophies of meaningful motion found in Material Design itself.

Results

My hope is for this presentation to inspire designers to not only utilize Material Design but to develop their own design language built on a foundation of excessive intention and information beautification.
