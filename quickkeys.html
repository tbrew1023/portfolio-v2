Quick Keys

Locksmith website

Project Summary

Quick Keys (Formerly Luke's Locks) Is a residential, commercial, and automotive locksmith based in St. Louis.

Creative Process

I was commissioned to build this site Summer of 2018, and it sort of became a playground for me to try out new techniques with CSS and JQuery. One of which was the raining effect in the main banner with the keys and locks. I began to focus a lot on experimentation with this site. I also designed the logo, replacing the old Luke's Lock's logo. I was going for a friendlier looking logo that's inviting, matching the tinderness of the locksmith himself. I tried to maintain that level of approachability throughout the entire site with an emphasis on cool blues, fun animations, and rounded corners, inspired by Google's material design philosophy.

Results

Even though this was a bit of a smaller project, I still learned so much about both CSS and JQuery just from messing around with new techniques.
