HBR Competencies

Cross-functional Knowledge Management Platform

Project Summary

HBR Competencies (HBRC) is a cross-functional knowledge management web application, and an internal enterprise platform that aims to encourage cross-selling and employee onboarding through gamified quizzes, and compilation of employee resources to encourage collaboration.

Creative Process

HBRC was built using Vue.js & SCSS on the front end, and Firebase & Node.js on the back end. My role in the project spans the full spectrum of the development process, leading the design and research phase, front-end implimentation, back-end development & information governance, authentication, task automation for bulk database imports/exports and deployment/maintenance.

Results

HBRC helped me to develop fluency with server-side javascript, data visualization using HTML Canvas & SVG, OAuth & SAML authentication, Azure Active Directory, Firebase Admin API, and leading a system's architecture and deployment.
